Connecting

1)Connect the included Thunderbolt cable to your computer and the other end of the cable to one of the Thunderbolt ports located on the rear of mTape.

2)Attach the power plug to the power jack on the rear of mTape and plug the power cord into a power outlet.

Powering On

1) Press the power button located on the back of the unit down to the “I” position.

2) Wait for the Green Ready Light on front of mTape to illuminate.

3) Power on your computer.

Disable Sleep Mode

It is necessary to disable your Mac’s sleep mode to ensure your Mac does not go to sleep when
